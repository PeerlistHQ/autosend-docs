## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step titleSize="h3" title="Install Server">
    Open Raycast and run the **Install Server** command.
  </Step>
  <Step titleSize="h3" title="Configure AutoSend">
    Enter the following details:

    - **Name:** `AutoSend`
    - **Transport:** HTTP
    - **URL:** `https://mcp.autosend.com/`

    Click **Install**.

  </Step>
  <Step titleSize="h3" title="Authorize">
    Follow the OAuth prompt to log in to your AutoSend account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
