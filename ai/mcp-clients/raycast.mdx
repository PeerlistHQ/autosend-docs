## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Server" titleSize="h3">
    Open Raycast and run the **Install Server** command.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure AutoSend" titleSize="h3">
    Enter the following details:

    - **Name:** `AutoSend`
    - **Transport:** HTTP
    - **URL:** `https://mcp.autosend.com/`

    Click **Install**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize" titleSize="h3">
    Follow the OAuth prompt to log in to your AutoSend account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
