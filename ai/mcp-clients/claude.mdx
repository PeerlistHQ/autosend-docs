## Claude Desktop

<Steps>
  <Step titleSize="h3" title="Open Connectors">
    Open Claude Desktop and click **Customize** in the top-left corner, then click **Connectors**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 11 46 25 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at11.46.25AM.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step titleSize="h3" title="Add AutoSend">
    Click the **\+** button and select **Add custom connector**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 11 51 48 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at11.51.48AM.png)
    </Frame>


  </Step>
  <Step titleSize="h3" title="Add custom connector">
    Enter the name as `AutoSend` and the URL as `https://mcp.autosend.com/`.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 11 53 48 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at11.53.48AM-1.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step titleSize="h3" title="Connect and Authorize">
    Click on the **Connect** and follow the OAuth prompt to log in to your AutoSend account.

    <Columns cols={2}>
      <Frame>
        ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 11 54 17 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at11.54.17AM-1.png)
      </Frame>
      <Frame>
        ![AutoSend OAuth Screen](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at11.56.14AM.png)
      </Frame>
    </Columns>

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
	Claude Desktop does not support remote MCP servers via the `claude_desktop_config.json` file. You
	must add remote servers through **Connectors**.
</Info>

## Claude Code

Run the following command in your terminal:

```bash
claude mcp add autosend --transport http https://mcp.autosend.com/
```

When you first use the MCP tools, Claude Code will open your browser to authorize the connection with your AutoSend account.
