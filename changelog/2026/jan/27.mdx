## What's New

### 1. Dashboard That Actually Shows What You Need

Your dashboard now surfaces everything important right when you log in:

- **Analytics at a glance**: Key metrics like sends, opens, clicks, and bounces front and center. No more digging through reports.
- **Active campaigns first**: See what's scheduled or running right now.
- **Product updates**: Stay in the loop with the latest features and improvements we've shipped, without checking our changelog manually.

The new dashboard is designed to answer your first question every time you log in: "What's happening with my emails?"

## More Improvements

### Faster CSV Imports

Importing large contact lists just got way faster. Upload your CSV and get back to work. We'll let you know when it's done.


### User ID Support in CSV Imports

You can now import user IDs along with your contact data. Perfect if you're syncing contacts from your own database and want to maintain consistent identifiers across systems.

Check the <a href={APP_PATHS.contactsImportCsv} target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">CSV Import Guide</a> for details.

### List-Unsubscribe Header

We've added the standard `List-Unsubscribe` header to all emails sent through AutoSend. This helps email clients like Gmail and Apple Mail show one-click unsubscribe buttons, improving deliverability and keeping you compliant with email best practices.

It just works. No configuration needed.
