## What's New

### Campaigns

Create and send one-time or scheduled marketing emails to your contacts, lists, or segments. Perfect for newsletters, announcements, and promotional emails.

**Key Features:**
- **HTML Email Designer**: Build responsive emails with inline CSS support
- **Variable Personalization**: Use variables like `{{first_name}}`, `{{company_name}}` to personalize content dynamically
- **Real-time Preview**: See how your email looks on desktop and mobile devices before sending
- **Send Now or Schedule**: Send campaigns immediately or schedule them for later
- **Test Emails**: Send test emails to up to 10 recipients before launching your campaign
- **Targeting Options**: Send to all contacts, specific lists, or dynamic segments
- **Exclusion Lists**: Exclude specific lists or segments from receiving campaigns

---

### Contacts Management

Organize and manage your audience with powerful contact management tools.

**Features:**
- **All Contacts View**: See every contact in your account in one place
- **Contact Details**: View comprehensive information including reserved fields, custom fields, lists, segments, unsubscribe status, and email activity
- **Reserved Fields**: System-defined fields like `email`, `firstName`, `lastName`, `mobile`, `address`, and more
- **Custom Fields**: Create custom fields (text, number, or date) to store additional information about your contacts
- **CSV Import**: Bulk import contacts via CSV with field mapping and automatic duplicate detection
- **Contact Activity**: Track email history and engagement for each contact

---

### Lists & Segments

Organize your contacts for better targeting and engagement.

**Lists:**
- Static collections of contacts grouped together
- Perfect for fixed criteria like "Newsletter Subscribers" or "Event Attendees"
- Add contacts manually or via CSV import

**Segments:**
- Dynamic groups that update automatically based on conditions
- Target audiences based on real-time data (e.g., users who signed up last month)
- Create segments from any list with custom criteria
- Auto-updates as contacts meet or stop meeting your defined criteria

---

### Sender Management

Add and manage sender emails for your marketing campaigns.

**Features:**
- Add sender names and email addresses
- Set reply-to addresses
- Only authenticated domains are allowed (ensures better deliverability)
- Build trust with recipients by using verified sender addresses

---

### Unsubscribe Groups

Categorize your emails so recipients can choose which types of emails they want to receive.

**Benefits:**
- **Better User Control**: Recipients can unsubscribe from specific email types without opting out of everything
- **Improved Deliverability**: Keep your lists cleaner and engagement rates higher
- **Compliance**: Supports CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and CASL regulations
- **Visibility Control**: Toggle visibility of unsubscribe groups on your email preferences page

**Use Cases:**
- Product Updates
- Newsletters
- Promotions
- Event Invites
