### **Key Features:**

- **Markdown Support**: Build emails using intuitive visual blocks or simple markdown, perfect for non-technical teams. No HTML knowledge required.

- **Clean HTML Export**: Export fully coded HTML, ready to plug into your ESP or backend. The exported code is production-ready and optimized for email clients.

- **Personalization & Variables**: Use variables and placeholders to personalize emails at scale. Perfect for transactional and marketing emails that need dynamic content.

- **Universal Compatibility**: Compatible with all major email clients, ensuring emails display correctly across platforms like Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, and Apple Mail.

- **Responsive Design**: Emails adapt perfectly across devices without the need for breakpoints or tweaks. Your templates will look great on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices automatically.

- **No Signup Required**: Start building immediately - no account creation or login needed. Just visit, create, and export.

Whether you're a developer looking to speed up email template creation, a marketer who wants to build emails without touching code, or a designer who needs clean HTML output, Compose by AutoSend makes email template creation effortless.
